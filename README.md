# sales_lead_data_visualization
#### Executive Summary
There are 4 different main sectinos to analyze the entire form. The first part list some basic statistics based on simple caculation. Those four key performance indicators provide us a high-level 'health check' of our sales funnel. And the second section conversion efficiency and distribution of different categories. Sepcifically, the left diagram compares the performance between "Group Connected" leads and "Individual" leads. It visualizes the drop-off rate from initial contact to final enrollment. While the right diagram showes the current status of leads across different student groups, helping us identify which segments are most active. The next section focus on the products and performance of customer representative. There are two different diagram. The first one is a visual distribution of the most popular insurance policies, showing which plans are currently dominating the market. And the other one is a performance leaderboard based on conversion rates, identifying which representatives are most effective at closing leads. For the last section, it analyzes the relationship between a lead's history and their current conversion status with the pie graph and histogram.
